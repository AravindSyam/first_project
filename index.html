About Us

Welcome to Steel India Corporation, The world calss steel structure material under one Roof

Our Vision

Steel Industry especially roofing and Structural industry are experiencing unprecedented levels of growth. Success in these industries cannot be presumed, only leaders with exceptional management skills, production skills, vision and drive can expect to rise on top.

Our Team

Steel India Corporation who has market presence of steel industry at Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Karnataka and Andhra Pradesh with manufacturing unit at Chennai and Kochi, very proudly provide Quality steel roofing and structural products and services to our valued customers under a very professional team.

Our Mission

The mission of Steel India Corporation is to help our customers succeed by providing them with low cost, high quality metal building components backed by consistent and reliable service that enables them to be more efficient, flexible and responsive to their customers.
